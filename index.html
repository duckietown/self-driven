Self-Driven 2018

ETH Zürich is a leader in the field of intelligent mobility.

We invite you to one afternoon to see the projects that the ETH Zürich students perform in the area of autonomy and self-driving cars in particular.

Special features:

  • Come see the demos for the projects from the Autonomous Mobility on Demand (AMOD) class using the Duckietown platform.
  • Come see the ETH Zürich team for AMZ, who develops an autonomous go-kart.

Opportunity for students to work in autonomy at ETH Zürich

There are plenty of opportunities for students at ETH to work on self-driving cars and other autonomous robots, such as quadrotors and quadrupeds. During this day you can see the available work at:

  • The Frazzoli group
  • The AMZ project

Moreover, there are other labs at ETH that work on autonomy. These include:

  • The Autonomous Systems Lab - prof. Siegwart
  • The Robotic Systems Lab - prof. Hutter
  • The Robotic Vision Lab - prof. Chli
  • Robotics Perception Group - prof. Scaramuzza
